ABOUT ME

Sadie Garner

My name is Sadie Garner and I am a 21-year-old aspiring crime writer living in Johannesburg. I was born in Edenvale, Johannesburg. I originally wanted to be a policewoman, but I decided I was too small to be able to overpower criminals. I then figured that I should still try to solve crimes from afar through crime writing.
