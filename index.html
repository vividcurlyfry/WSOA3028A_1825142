ABOUT ME

Sadie Garner

My name is Sadie Garner and I am a 21-year-old aspiring crime writer living in Johannesburg. I was born in Edenvale, Johannesburg. I originally wanted to be a policewoman, but then I realised the crime solving done by cops in this country is less than desired. Also, I'm a bit too small to overpower criminals. I then figured that I should still try to solve crimes from afar through crime writing.

I gained an interest in true crime when an acquaintance in my school was kidnapped. The girl was walking home from school when a man and wife in a van grabbed her. There was only one witness. The case has never been solved and has gone cold.

I have created this website in order to help solve what I call the "Township Killer" Case. Under "Victims" you can find all the victims and information about the murders and kidnappings. Please contact me with any information you may have about these murders. Under "Victims" you can sign up to get emails everytime there is an update in the case!

Please note. This is a fictional website. None of the cases or people mentioned are real.
